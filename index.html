The recipe of pancakes

  • Time: 1 hour

  • Portion: 1

  • Difficulty: 1

    Ingridients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of cooking oil

    Kitchen tools

  • Tea and table spoon
  • Cup
  • Pan
  • Spatula

Operation

1) Combine and mix all the ingredients well in a large bowl and after adding baking powder to dough - do not stir the dough and leave for 10 minutes until it rises

2) Spoon the oil onto the hot frying pan; cook on both sides until golden brown.

3) Serve the pancakes with honey or jam, or sour cream and eat with drinking coffee or tea! And admire the taste :)
